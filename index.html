Understanding how NMN supports your cellular health and longevity

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NMN and Uthful Wellness

What is NMN and how does it work? +
NMN (Nicotinamide Mononucleotide) is a direct precursor to NAD+, a coenzyme essential for cellular energy production, DNA repair, and healthy aging. When you take NMN, your body converts it into NAD+, helping replenish levels that naturally decline with age.
How long does it take to see results? +
Many customers report increased energy and mental clarity within 2-4 weeks. For full cellular benefits, we recommend consistent use for at least 3 months. Individual results vary based on age, lifestyle, and baseline NAD+ levels.
Is NMN safe? Are there side effects? +
NMN is generally well-tolerated and considered safe for healthy adults. Some people may experience mild digestive discomfort when starting. As with any supplement, consult your healthcare provider before use, especially if pregnant, nursing, or taking medications.
What's the difference between 500mg and 1000mg? +
The 500mg is ideal for beginners or maintenance dosing. The 1000mg provides a higher dose for those seeking maximum NAD+ support, athletes, or individuals over 50. Both use the same pharmaceutical-grade NMN.
How should I store my NMN supplements? +
Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Our bottles are designed to protect against moisture and light. Refrigeration is not necessary but can extend shelf life. Always keep the desiccant packet in the bottle.
